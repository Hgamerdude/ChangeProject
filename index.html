Don't waste Food


2.5 billion tons of food are wasted each year according to the World Resource Institute. We need to do something about it!

Solution 1

Many people throw away food that is still good to eat. This can be because they forget about it, or they don't know how to store it properly. To help reduce food waste, we can try to plan our meals better and only buy what we need. We can also learn how to store food properly so that it lasts longer. Additionally, we can donate any extra food we have to local food banks or shelters. Click here to learn more

Solution 2

We can also compost food scraps instead of throwing them away. This helps reduce the amount of waste that goes to landfills and provides nutrient-rich soil for gardening.

Solution 3

Another solution is to support local farmers and food producers. By buying locally grown food, we can reduce the carbon footprint associated with transporting food long distances. Additionally, supporting local farmers helps to sustain the local economy and promotes sustainable farming practices.
